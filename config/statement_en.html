The Goal

Guess the correct word before your opponent.

  Rules

The game is played between two players. Both player have their own board and play simultaneously. The game is played on turns. On each turn, both player should output a word.

  • Reveling a letter present in the word will get you 2 points
  • Reveling a letter and it's position will get you 5 points
  • Guessing the correct word will get you a score of 50

N.B: The word not necessarily be a real word for the league only.
Victory Conditions
  • You guess correct before your opponent
  • You score more than your opponent
In case of tie, the game is considered as a draw, with both player being 1st
Defeat Conditions
  • Your opponent guess correct before your
  • Your opponent score more than you

  Note

The program must, within an infinite loop, read the contextual data from the standard input and provide to the standard output the desired instructions.

  Game Input

The program must first read the initialization data from standard input. Then, provide to the standard output one line for each game turn.
Initializaton input

Line 1: An integer wordCount, total number of words in the word set.

Line 2: wordCount space separated word of length 5, represents the word set from where the word will be chosen.

Input for one game turn

A single line 5 space separated integer, representing the state of the letter of respective position of your previos guess

A letter can hvae four states:
  • 1 means the letter is not present in the word
  • 2 means the letter is present in the word but position is incorrect
  • 3 means the letter is present in the word and position is correct
  • 0 means the letter's state is unknown
For the first turn all the 5 states are 0
Output
A single line containing a word of length 5 in uppercase
